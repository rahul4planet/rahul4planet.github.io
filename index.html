About Me

Research Journey

Currently pursuing my PhD in Environmental Science with a focus on climate change impacts on terrestrial ecosystems. My research combines field studies, remote sensing data, and predictive modeling to understand how environmental changes affect biodiversity and ecosystem services.

Adaptable researcher skilled in handling high demands and multitasking. Leveraging expertise in atmospheric sciences, climate change, remote sensing and GIS. I specialize in using R and Python for analyzing climatic data, with a focus on water security and climate change through CMIP6 models, meteorological information, and remote sensing. My background in remote sensing allows me to interpret geospatial data, applying these skills to process large datasets and model climate-related factors to support sustainable decision-making.
